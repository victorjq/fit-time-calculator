About

FitTime gives you a simple estimate of how long it might take to reach a goal. It uses common formulas, safe weekly rates, and a diet quality factor so results feel practical, not perfect.

What you get: a time range that respects healthy progress. What you don’t get: medical advice or promises. Use this as a planning guide, then adjust based on sleep, stress, training, and consistency.

  • Fat loss with safe weekly caps
  • Muscle gain with realistic rates
  • 5k improvement windows
  • Diet quality adjusts the pace
  • No accounts, nothing saved to a server

    How it works

    Estimate your burn

    We calculate BMR with Mifflin St Jeor, then multiply by your activity level to estimate daily energy needs.

    Set a safe pace

    Fat loss respects healthy weekly limits. Muscle gain uses realistic weekly rates based on typical progress.

    Factor in diet

    Diet quality affects speed. Balanced nutrition sustains progress. Low protein or fast food slows it down.

    Show a clear window

    You get a time range, not a promise, so you can plan, track, and adjust as life happens.
